Music Production

Music production has been a passion of mine for roughly a year now. I have always loved music, I listen to it every single day. It was only a matter of time that I took the initiative to begin producing my own music.

I taught myself how to play guitar years ago. Although it was enjoyable, it didn't quite fulfill my desire for music creation, which is when I began to look into music production using FL Studio.

FL Studio is a DAW software that allows one to use, organize and arrange different sound samples and plug-ins to create music. It definitely has a steep learning curve to it as there is an immense amount of features and tools to learn about.

I have recently been uploading my beats to SoundCloud. Currently I only do this as a hobby but I would love to be able to find a career in music production.

I thought about changing my major to something music related, but I feared it would turn my passion to work and I would begin to lose interest.

Since music is an art, I never rush beats. I only work on music when I am in the mood to. I find this is when I produce the best.

For those interested, you can check out some of my beats on my SoundCloud and my Instagram.
